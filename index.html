About Me


I’m a Game Designer with a Bachelors of Science in Game Design & Development and a minor in Software Engineering from Rochester Institute of Technology. I’ve had a passion for level design since I was a kid and especially love creating multiplayer environments for both casual and competitive play. I’ve taken an interest in User Generated Content and believe that it’s a fantastic way to grow a portfolio and have players discover my work easily.

I've had past experiences with being a Senior Level Designer for Minecraft servers where I could control the gameplay and environments. Knowing millions of players have stepped foot into my creations and enjoyed a new experience every time has me wanting even more of my work to be displayed to the world. These servers have allowed me to also coordinate large scale events with thousands of players participating in tournaments containing some of my work.
